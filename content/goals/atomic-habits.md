## Key Takeaways

1. **1% better every day** - Small improvements compound over time
2. **Systems over goals** - Focus on the process, not the outcome
3. **Identity-based habits** - Become the type of person who does X

## Favorite Quote

> "Every action you take is a vote for the type of person you wish to become."

## Would Recommend To

Anyone looking to build better habits or understand the psychology of behavior change.
